Republic Act No. 9344>

R.A. 9344 Information>

  • Republic Act No. 9344, also known as the Juvenile Justice and Welfare Act of 2006, is a law in the Philippines that governs the treatment of children in conflict with the law.

    • The key provisions of R.A. 9344 include:

    • Diversion of children in conflict with the law from the regular court processes
    • Establishment of the Local Councils for the Protection of Children
    • Creation of the Juvenile Justice and Welfare Council
    • Promotion of restorative justice and rehabilitation of children in conflict with the law
